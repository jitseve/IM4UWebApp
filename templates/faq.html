Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to use IM4U WebApp?

You need to be in possesion of a movesense inertial measurement unit. These can be bought on movesense.com

What does IM4U do?

Currently, using data from your movesense sensor we can indicate your step frequencey
Also we can determine your ground contact times during a sprint. All done with a single sensor on your shoe.
